Our Journey Started in 2018

Founded with a vision to transform intercity travel in East Africa, SwiftRides began as a small startup with a big dream. We recognized the challenges travelers faced with unreliable transport, poor service quality, and safety concerns.

Today, we've grown into the region's most trusted transport network, connecting major cities across Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, and Burundi. Our success is built on three pillars: safety, comfort, and reliability.
